About me.

I am a graduate student, under the supervision of Adam Topaz. My research interests lie in Algebraic Geometry, Category Theory and Combinatorics.

I was previously at the University of Waterloo where I completed my BMATH in Pure Mathematics, and joint Combinatorics and Optimization.

I also work on formalization of mathematics in Lean and visualising maths with Rust. You can find more of this work on my github: razafy-rindra.
